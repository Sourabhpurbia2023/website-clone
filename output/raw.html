Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using BlitzAPI. Still have questions? Drop us a message — we’ll get back to you quickly.

What exactly is BlitzAPI?

BlitzAPI is a collection of high-performance B2B data APIs designed specifically for Growth, Revenue, and Operations teams. From ICP targeting and contact enrichment to email validation, our APIs provide real-time, verified data at the lowest possible cost.

What does "at scraping cost" mean?

It means our pricing is comparable to manual scraping—extremely affordable, but with data that’s already cleaned, verified, and ready to use. You get high-quality B2B data without the hassle, risk, or overhead of managing scraping yourself.

Do I need a developer or technical background to use BlitzAPI?

Not necessarily. While BlitzAPI is designed for technical teams comfortable with APIs, it integrates seamlessly into no-code tools like Make, Zapier, N8N, and Airtable. Complete documentation, examples, and step-by-step tutorials are provided.

What specific use cases can BlitzAPI handle for Growth teams?

BlitzAPI supports unlimited growth use cases including ICP-targeted outreach, automated enrichment of leads, advanced lead scoring, email validation at scale, and firmographic enrichment from company domains.

How quickly can I get started with BlitzAPI?

Immediately! Upon signup, you receive 100 free credits to test all APIs. Integration typically takes just minutes, especially if you use popular automation platforms or follow our clear documentation.

What integrations and tools work best with BlitzAPI?

BlitzAPI integrates effortlessly with popular growth and automation tools, including Make, Zapier, N8N, Clay, Salesforce, HubSpot, Airtable, Google Sheets, and internal custom scripts.

Is BlitzAPI GDPR compliant?

Yes. BlitzAPI ensures compliance by never storing or processing sensitive personal data unnecessarily. All data provided is publicly sourced, professionally validated and meeting GDPR standards.

Can I request a specific feature or additional API?

Absolutely—we’re always open to user feedback and specific requests. If you have a particular feature, data enrichment type, or integration in mind, let us know, and we’ll explore adding it to our roadmap.
